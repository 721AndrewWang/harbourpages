from scratch, full stop

Reviewers put it best: food genuinely cooked from scratch, the kind that tastes like someone's mother made it. Plantain stew sits beside Guinness beef stew — the board wanders, the standard doesn't.

tea in real china

No paper cups, no fancy coffees. French press brewed slowly, loose-leaf tea poured into proper china — because a market Saturday deserves a table you want to stay at.

the market's hidden room

Look for us at the back of Blackrock Market, past the stalls. Seats inside and out — and the small room fills quickly on Saturday mornings, so booking ahead is the wise move.

Good to know, from your public listings: vegan and vegetarian options on the board · dogs have been welcomed at the market tables · no restroom in the restaurant itself (the market's is steps away). All corrected on day one if we've got anything wrong.
