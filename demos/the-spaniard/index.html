III.

The story

1601 — the battle

Don Juan del Águila landed four thousand men at Kinsale and fought the English alongside Irish allies. The English won the day — but the Don won the folklore, and three and a half centuries later the inn on the hill took his name.

c.1650 — the inn

Raised on the foundations of an old castle at Scilly, it poured for sailors and fishermen almost from the start — some of the first patrons were likely veterans of the battle itself. The beams are original. The welcome, too.
